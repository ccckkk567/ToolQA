Action 1: sql_interpreter[SELECT departure_time FROM flights WHERE date='2022-06-05' AND origin='MIA' AND destination='LAS' AND flight_number='AA2319']
Observation 1: [("21:43",)]
Thought 2: 数据库返回了结果 "21:43"，这就是该航班的起飞时间。我已经得到了答案。
Action 2: finish[21:43]
